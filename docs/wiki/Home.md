## ðŸŒŸ What is "For My Valentine"?

"For My Valentine" is a static web application that allows users to generate a customized link for their partner. When the partner opens the link, they are greeted with a personalized experience, including:

-   **Personalized Greetings:** Addressing them by name.
-   **Vibe Match:** A fun name-based compatibility calculator.
-   **Date Planner:** A multi-step process to pick food, location, and activities.
-   **Response Capture:** Responses are saved locally and can optionally be sent to your email.
